A process designed so that each family feels supported from day one.

Create the family

Create or join a family using secure codes.

Care and reminders

Manage medication and check the notification history from a secure dashboard.

Implementation checklist

  • Quick registration and secure verification.
  • Family creation and role assignment.
  • Invitations by email or protected link.
  • Medication, routines and event setup.
  • Daily use with alerts and confirmations.
