Gerontechnology

Science and evidence

DiAlgo applies gerontechnology principles to improve treatment adherence and social connectivity. Its design reduces the cognitive load on the dependent user and facilitates coordination between formal caregivers and families.

Minimal cognitive load

Simple interactions and clear messages for users with lower digital literacy.

Adherence support

Recurring reminders and visible confirmations for the entire support network.

Social connectivity

Constant channels to reduce isolation and strengthen family bonds.
