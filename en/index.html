Security

Security and privacy

Sensitive information is protected with robust authentication, role-based permissions and encryption in transit.

Key measures

  • Secure authentication (email and trusted providers).
  • Strict role and family rules in the database.
  • TLS encryption in transit and encryption at rest in Firebase.
  • Activity log for auditing.
  • Data minimization: only what is essential.

Compliance and transparency

  • GDPR compliance and explicit consent.
  • Clear and accessible privacy policy.
  • Transparent management of permissions and access to logs.
